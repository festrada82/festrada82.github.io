About Me

Build Test Improve. Keep moving forward.

Hands-on Mechanical Designer & Prototyper

I’m a mechanical designer and prototyper who has been fixing and building things since age seven—when I repaired my mother’s sewing machine and discovered the joy of making hardware work. Today, I blend industrial design, manufacturing know-how, and electronics to turn ideas into clean, manufacturable CAD and working prototypes.

I’m a Certified SOLIDWORKS Professional (CSWP + CSWPA) and have taught advanced SOLIDWORKS for 13+ years (parametric parts/assemblies, sheet metal, surfacing, drawings, and CAM). My workflow emphasizes DFM, clear feature trees, versioned files, and practical iteration.

Selected builds & reverse engineering

  • Medical ventilator prototype (2020) — full assembly, mechanisms, and documentation.
  • CNC machines — briefcase-size mill (2012) and a custom CNC plasma cutter (mechanics + controls).
  • Bean cleaner with computer vision, harness-taping machine, sheet-metal bender, and a seed roaster with a custom gas-flow sensor and control.
  • Reverse-engineered parts — industrial camera, automotive components, replacement plastic parts.
  • Mechatronics — ESP32 + PLC integration for smarter prototypes.

What you get

  • Parametric CAD that’s easy to modify, production-ready drawings, and CAM-aware models.
  • Prototypes that actually work: mechanisms tuned, tolerances addressed, documentation delivered.
  • A partner who spots risks early and suggests simpler, more reliable ways to build.
